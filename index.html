HOW TO PLAY

MOVE: WASD  JUMP: Space  SPRINT: Shift  LOOK: Mouse

ATTACK: Left Click  PLACE BLOCK: Right Click  MINE: Hold Left Click with pickaxe

HOTBAR: 1-9 or scroll  SHOP: E near gold block  USE ITEM: Q

EARNING RESOURCES

Walk up to a Challenge Station (glowing block) on your island and press E.

Solve the math problem or spell the word correctly to earn resources!

Iron Station — easy problems, earn iron
Gold Station — medium problems, earn gold
Diamond Station — hard problems, earn diamonds

Use resources at the Shop ($) to buy blocks, weapons, and armor.

Build a bridge to the enemy island and destroy their EGG to win!
